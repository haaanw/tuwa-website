- Receive prescribed workouts that load directly into your session with target placeholders
- Unlink from your coach at any time -- revokes all access immediately
- You stay in control: coaching enhances your training, it doesn't take over

"For coaches" (h2):
- See every athlete's daily readiness at a glance. Red/yellow/green zones tell you who needs lighter work today
- Review ACWR trends to spot athletes approaching dangerous load spikes
- Log workouts on behalf of athletes during in-person sessions
- Build workout templates and assign them to individuals or groups

- Manage multiple athletes from one coach account
- Each athlete's data is isolated -- athletes cannot see each other's scores
